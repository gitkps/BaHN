BaHN Automation was established in the year 1999 as a private limited company by a group of expert engineers having vast experience in Electrical / Electronic Products & Systems.  The key strength of the manpower is in Design & Development, Product Engineering, Project Execution, Marketing, Testing & Commissioning of various Electrical & Electronic Systems for diverse application areas viz., Cement, Paper, Steel, Rolling Stock for Railways and other application areas.

We have necessary expertise in design & development of state-of- the-art custom and standard “Electronic Products & Systems” based on latest hardware and software as per customer requirements. We have supplied technologically intensive “Electronic Products & Systems” for Indian Railways, GTRE, ISRO, John F Welch Technology Center India, Optimum Air Solutions Pvt. Ltd., and others as detailed below:-

  • Traction Electronic Sub-Systems
  • Analog Control Systems
  • Digital Control Systems
  • Embedded Control Systems
  • PLC Based Energy Optimization Systems.

BaHN Automation uses modern and powerful design tools and methodologies. Mathematical modeling and software simulation techniques are used to the maximum extent possible in design and development of a product. We incorporate total quality control at every stage of design, prototyping and final manufacturing process.

BaHN Automation draws its strength from a pool of talented, highly experienced, well-qualified engineers and dedicated associates.

Brief Profile of Our Directors 
Mr. K. P. Sundararajan

Mr. Sundararajan is the Company's Managing Director who brings with him more than 35 years of experience and expertise in the field of Power Electronics, Control Electronics, Industrial Automation and Software. During his long service with Larsen & Tubro and NGEF, he held key positions as the Head of Thyristor Drives Division, Head of Control Electronics Division and in the Steel Plant Division.

He was instrumental in setting up the Automation division in NGEF. As the head of Automation Division he was fully responsible for the development of Microtrac, India's first Microprocessor based Traction Controller for Kolkata-Metro, developed entirely with indigenous know-how.

He also played a major role in the development of many on board electronic items for Metro Railway, Kolkata and development of various Control Electronics Systems for Liquid & Electric Propulsion Systems for LPSC (ISRO).

Prof. V. Ramanarayanan

Prof. Ramanarayanan was the former Chairman and Head of Department of Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. He holds a doctorate in Power Electronics from California Institute of Technology, USA.

He has vast industrial experience, having held key positions in Larsen & Tubro and NGEF, as the Head of Inverter Division and as the Head of Power Electronics (R & D) Division. Prof. Ramanarayanan was also a Technology Consultant to number of reputed organizations in India. Prof. Ramanarayanan is a Director of our company.

Mr. V. Satyaprakash

Mr. Satyaprakash is the Director of the company, having more than 35 years of industrial experience, in the field of manufacturing and management of Electrical, Electronic, Electro-mechanical items.

He had held very responsible positions in various organizations like, Hegde & Golay, AEG-NGEF, NGEF Ltd., (in Collaboration with AEG, Germany), as General manager of Measurement Systems Pvt. Ltd., (in collaboration with Powertron of UK) and as Vice-President, Manufacturing, RANN International, USA (in collaboration of Techno Ame Co. of Japan)

Mr. C. M. Dinakar

Mr. Dinakar is the Director of the company, having more than 35 years of experience, in the field of Electrical Design, Thyristor Drive Systems, Application, System Engineering and Turnkey projects.

He had held responsible positions in reputed Electrical Manufacturing & Contracting companies like, NGEF, Kirloskar Electric Co., Dodsal Ltd., ABB Electric Industries, ABB Contracting Co., Riyadh.

Mr. S. K. Nagaraja

Mr. Nagaraja is the Vice President of the company, having more than 30 years of experience and expertise in Power Electronics, Industrial Automation and Turnkey Projects. During his initial service of 5 years in R&D Dept., of Best & Crompton Engg. Ltd., Chennai, he was involved in design, development & commissioning of power electronics equipments and microprocessor based elevator control systems.

During his long service of 20 years in NGEF Ltd., he was involved in tendering & execution of turnkey electrical projects including automation for various Process Industries as well as Rolling Stock Equipment's for Railways. He also played a key role in engineering, software development & commissioning of Microtrac, India's first Microprocessor based Traction Controller for Kolkata-Metro.
