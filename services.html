Multiple Compressors System Control (MCSC)

BaHN Automation worked as a system integrator to M/s.Optimum Air Solutions Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore in design and development of “Multiple Compressors System Control (MCSC)” supplied by M/s.OptimAir.

BaHN Automation is basically involved in design & development of custom products and systems. Subsequent to the closure of M/s OptimAir in April' 2011. their customers have been approaching BaHN Automation for support and servicing of MCSC Systems.

Although BaHN Automation is fully engaged in various other assignments currently, in order to help the customers of M/s OptimAir for trouble shooting of MCSC Systems, BaHN Automation proposes to offer one time support and services for a limited period, as listed below:-

1. Deputation of Service Team

  • Trouble shooting & tuning of MCSC system
  • Training of O&M personnel.

2. Supply of Software, Documentation & Spares

  • Supply of MCSC Software (PLC Ladder & Touch Panel)
  • Supply of Complete Documentation viz., O&M Manual, Drawings etc.,
  • Supply of Compressor Interface Modules & any other spare

 

The above support and services will be offered by BaHN Automation only on chargeable basis up to March' 2012. As BaHN Automation has embarked on new business avenues, any support and services related to OptimAir MCSC Systems beyond March' 2012, would be subject to availability of suitable resources at that time.
