Apart from Import Substitution Products, Custom Products & Systems, BaHN has designed and developed number of standard "Electronics Products & Systems" for diverse application areas.

“Multiple Compressor System Control (MCSC)” is a unique product developed by BaHN based on a patented concept of M/s.Fouress Company LLC, USA as an “Energy Optimization System for Compressed Air Management”. Number of MCSC systems have been supplied to diverse manufacturing and process industries in India and Abroad thro’ M/s. Optimum Air Solutions Pvt. Ltd., resulting in energy savings of 15% to 30% per annum.

“MultiScope – Multi Channel Storage Scope / Data Logger” is the latest product developed by BaHN based on proven USB-DAQ hardware. This product has functional features of storage scope as well as data logger for 8 / 16 / 32 channel requirements with user defined configuration options.

“DC Isolating Amplifier” developed by BaHN, converts high voltage analog signals to low voltage signals. The analog input voltage is converted into digital signal using advanced Sigma- Delta conversion techniques, transferred across an isolation barrier optically and converted back into analog form with an isolation level of 3000V.

Wide range of “DC Power Supplies” is also manufactured by BaHN. “GSM Switch” developed by one of our associates is another standard product promoted by BaHN.
