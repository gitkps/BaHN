Defence - GTRE, Bangalore

BaHN Automation was privileged to associate with Gas Turbine Research Establishment (GTRE), Bangalore as a supplier for indigenous design and development of Analog Control Systems for their Aero Engine’s Simulation Rig Facilities.

BaHN's endeavor resulted in indigenous development of following “Analog Control Systems” for testing of KAVERI engine being developed by GTRE for Navy & Combat Aircrafts

  1. Analog Control Box for Reheat Fuel System
  2. Analog Control Box for Main Engine Control Unit (MECU)
  3. Controller for Burner Down Stream Pressure (BDSP)
  4. Upgradation of BDSP Controller ACB
  5. DC Power Supply for Jet Fuel Starter (JFS) Motor

  6. Test & Calibration Unit for Pressure Transducers.
  7. Programmable Pulse Generator for Testing of Solenoid Valves.
  8. Digital Interface Units for Pressure Transducers
  9. Simulation Test Data Acquisition System (SiTe DAS).
  10. Tank & PAS Testing Data Acquisition System (TPT DAS).
  11. Portable & Real Data Acquisition System (PRT DAS).
  12. Control Electronics Module for Pulsed Plasma Thruster (CEM PPT)

Industrial-John F Welch Technology, Bangalore

BaHN Automation has supplied following custom built electronic items to John F Welch Technology Center, for their R&D activities.

1. Corrosion Alarm Unit

The main features of this unit are as under:-

  • Selection of corrosion resistance
  • Detection of corrosion
  • Time counting in days, hours, min & secs
  • Elapsed time indication on digital meter

2. High Voltage DC Power Supply

The main features of this unit are as under:-

  • Variac controlled using servo motor
  • Input Voltage : 415Vac+/-15%, 3-Ph, 50Hz, 4-Wire
  • Output Voltage : 0 to 2kV
  • Output Current : 10A
