Defence - GTRE, Bangalore

BaHN Automation was privileged to associate with Gas Turbine Research Establishment (GTRE), Bangalore as a supplier for indigenous design and development of Analog Control Systems for their Aero Engine’s Simulation Rig Facilities.

 BaHN's endeavor resulted in indigenous development of following “Analog Control Systems” for testing of KAVERI engine being developed by GTRE for Navy & Combat Aircrafts

  1. Analog Control Box for Reheat Fuel System
  2. Analog Control Box for Main Engine Control Unit (MECU)
  3. Controller for Burner Down Stream Pressure (BDSP)
  4. Upgradation of BDSP Controller ACB
  5. DC Power Supply for Jet Fuel Starter (JFS) Motor
